Tania Chapper is a dedicated psychotherapist with over 15 years of experience supporting individuals through their mental health challenges. Holding a Master's in Psychology and certifications in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) and Brainspotting, Tania is deeply committed to helping her clients heal from trauma, anxiety, depression, and other emotional struggles using proven, evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

Tania is also a certified Yoga Instructor, bringing an integrated approach to mental health by combining psychotherapy with body-centered healing practices. With a background in dance, she understands the profound connection between the mind and body and uses this insight to guide her clients through movement therapy, yoga, and embodiment practices to promote emotional and physical well-being.

Having worked both in the United States and Mexico, Tania brings a unique, cross-cultural perspective to her work, particularly in assisting expats and individuals navigating life transitions, cultural adjustments, and complex personal challenges. Her compassionate and holistic approach aims to empower her clients, helping them reclaim their sense of self, restore emotional balance, and achieve their goals.
